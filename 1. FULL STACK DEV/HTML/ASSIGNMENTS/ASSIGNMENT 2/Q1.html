PW Skills

Physics Wallah Private Limited, commonly known as "Physics Wallah" or "PW", was founded in 2016 as a YouTube Channel by Alakh Pandey, an educator hailing from Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh. Along with co-founder Prateek Maheshwari, Pandey developed an app specifically designed for students.

Why PW Skills ?

  2. Affordable : Courses are present at affordable price ranges.
  3. Experienced Mentors : Mentors are field specialist and highly educated on industry demands
